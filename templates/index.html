Manage Connections

You can manage your connections from the sidebar:

  • Edit: Click the icon next to a connection.
  • Reorder: Drag and drop connections to rearrange efficiently.
  • Delete: Use the delete option in the edit menu.
  • Backup: Use the "Backup Connections" option in your profile menu.
